Driven By Passion

Built on Passion

Adorable Christmas Charity Foundation was initiated in 2014 by Dame Adaora Umeorji, the current Group Managing Director (GMD) of Zenith Bank Plc.
Motivated by her passion for uplifting the less privileged in society, Dame Adaora established the foundation with a core group known as Team 20. This group embraced her vision of caring for less privileged children, orphans, widows, and the downtrodden.
Since its inception, the foundation has remained steadfast in its mission, carrying out various impactful interventions, particularly in Abuja and Anambra states. Over the years, the foundation has grown to become a beacon of hope, spreading care, joy, and happiness among underserved communities.

Vision: To bring hope, health, and happiness to the less privileged in society through impactful interventions.
